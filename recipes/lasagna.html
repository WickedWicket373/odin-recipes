Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomatos
  • Sugar
  • Cheeses
  • Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Cook Meat, Onion and Garlic in a pan/pot.
  2. Stir Tomatos and Sugar together for the sauce
  3. Boil the noodles for 8 - 10 minutes.
  4. Assemple Lasagna and cook for a little while longer
